Biography

Executive Leadership

Dr. Audrin Mathe is the Executive Director (Permanent Secretary) of the Ministry of Information and Communication Technology. He previously served as Executive Director of Namibia's Ministry of Sport, Youth and National Service and as Chief Executive Officer of the New Era Publications Corporation.

Professional Experience

With over three decades of experience, Dr. Mathe has practised as a television producer, spokesperson and public commentator. He has written extensively on Leadership, Government and Political Communication, Rhetoric and Development policy.
